Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device. We don't sell your information, track your location history, or share your vehicle details with third parties.

Information We Collect

Vehicle Information

Year, make, model, and mileage you provide during setup. Stored locally on your device.

Chat Messages

Conversations with Clutch AI are stored locally and sent to Google Gemini API for processing. Messages are not stored on Google's servers beyond processing.

Photos

Quote and damage photos are processed by Google Gemini API for analysis. Photos are not stored on external servers.

Location

Used only when you access Service Locator to find nearby mechanics. We don't store or track your location history.

Service Records

Maintenance history you enter is stored locally on your device only.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI-powered automotive assistance
  • Analyze repair quotes and damage photos
  • Show nearby service locations
  • Track your vehicle's maintenance history
  • Improve app functionality

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification (name, address, phone)
  • Payment information
  • Driving behavior or trip history
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Background location tracking

Third-Party Services

Google Gemini AI

Powers our chat and image analysis features. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Apple MapKit

Powers the Service Locator. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage & Security

All personal data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. We do not maintain external databases of user information. Your data is protected by your device's security features including encryption and biometric locks.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all app data via Settings > Reset App
  • Revoke location permissions in iOS Settings
  • Clear chat history at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

Children's Privacy

Clutch is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
